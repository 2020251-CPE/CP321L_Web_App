<br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; The Self Reflection Station (SRS) website application provides a convenient, accessible, and user-friendly platform for engaging in regular self-reflection. The SRS website aims to help users develop self-awareness, improve their emotional regulation, and ultimately support them in leading more fulfilling and satisfying lives. The SRS website application seeks to address the barriers that prevent individuals from engaging in regular self-reflection by providing a range of interactive exercises, personalized feedback, and recommendations based on users' responses. By fulfilling this product purpose, the SRS website application aims to empower individuals to take control of their personal growth and well-being.The SRS website application seeks to improve users' emotional regulation, which can lead to reduced symptoms of anxiety and depression. Additionally, regular self-reflection can improve decision-making and interpersonal relationships.
